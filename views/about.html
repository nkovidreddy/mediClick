About MediClick

MediClick is a medical website designed to provide many medical services in a few clicks. Various features of MediClick include:

  • Symptom Checker

    The user can select the specific body part in which he/she is experiencing the symptoms using a human body model and can specify the symptoms in simple phrases in the Symptom Checker. MediClick uses Concept Extraction Techniques to extract the symptoms from the user entered symptoms and provides a list of possible conditions based on them.

  • Know Your Condition

    MediClick provides interesting and informative facts about the conditions provided, based on the symptoms entered by the users.

  • Insurance based doctor retrieval

    This is one of the unique features of MediClick. The user can enter his medical problem and select his Insurance plan and accordingly get a filtered list of doctors in the increasing order of distance based on his location. The users can view the ratings of the doctors, read more information about them, get their contact information and also book an appointment with the doctor.

  • Get Pharmacy

    Users can get navigated to their nearest pharmacies based on their location.

  • Emergency Alert System

    On clicking the ambulance button on the Home page, the user gets navigated to the nearest hospital and an alert message is sent to the emergency contacts set up by the user.

  • Caloriemeter

    This is an additional feature of MediClick which provides a diet plan based on the BMI of the user.
